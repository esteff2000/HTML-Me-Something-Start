Where It All Began


My name is Eleanor Steffenson, welcome to my story of how I've gotten to where I am. I was born and raised (and still currently live) in the suburbs of St. Louis, MO
When I had first began school, it had become my dream to get into a nursing program so I could become a pediatric nurse. However, life had other plans for me as I fought my way through college
Despite not going into nursing school, I still continued with my passion for healthcare. In 2022, I graduated from the University of Missouri with a Bachelors of Science in Health Sciences - Health and Wellness Services.




Throughout my time at the University of Missouri, I learned a lot about myself and discovered that I sought to be that person that people could turn to in times of need.
This dedication to be in a field where I can be the one to help others led me to the current field of work that I have been in since graduating in 2022.

My Journey So Far

When I graduated in 2022, I had no idea what I was going to do with my life. However, what I did know is I wanted to spread my message that I have been telling myself for as long as I can remember: "Always be kind, sometimes kindness is enough. Sometimes, kindness is all you can give"

Keeping this in mind, I am currently working with children in a preschool setting that uses the "Montessori Method"
